Village Services

Facilities and Community Services Available in Boobak

Education

Boobak has a well-established educational setup, consisting of several institutions including boys’ and girls’ high schools, multiple primary schools for both genders, and a local college. These institutions play an important role in providing basic to higher-level education within the village and surrounding areas, contributing significantly to literacy and academic development.

Religious Education

Boobak is a village with a strong religious and educational background, where many households are engaged in Islamic learning and scholarship. It is home to two key institutions, Madrasa Syedna Ibn Masood and Jamia Dar-ul-Arqam Boobak , which are supervised by respected figures including **Muhammad Shuaib** and Mufti Bakht Zada. The area has produced several well-known scholars such as Maulana Abdul Haq, Maulana Shams-ul-Haq, Maulana Misbah-ur-Rahman, Maulana Mufti Shoaib, and Maulana Syed Ali, along with many others serving in different religious roles. A notable contributor to the community was Maulana Abdul Rahman (late), who passed away in 2016 and dedicated around 40–45 years of his life to religious and social service in Boobak, leaving a lasting legacy.

Electricity

In terms of electricity, the village has a somewhat limited grid system; however, this challenge has been effectively managed through widespread solar energy adoption. Almost every household has installed solar systems, ensuring a continuous 24-hour electricity supply across the village. This energy is also utilized for agricultural purposes, such as running tube wells for irrigation, making the system both practical and highly efficient

Clean Water

In our village, Qatar Charity has made a remarkable contribution by installing water pumps to provide clean and safe drinking water to the community. This initiative has significantly improved access to water, especially for households facing shortages. It reflects a strong commitment to humanitarian support and sustainable community development. The effort is highly appreciated and has brought meaningful relief to local residents.

Road Network

Boobak is strategically located between Nowshera, Charsadda, and Mardan, making travel to nearby cities highly convenient. The village has strong road connectivity, allowing easy access to multiple urban centers. One of its major advantages is the proximity of the **M-1 Motorway**, which passes through the region. This provides fast and efficient travel to Peshawar and other major destinations. From Boobak, residents can easily reach Nowshera, Risalpur, and surrounding areas through well-connected road networks. Overall, the village is supported by a well-developed road system, making transportation smooth, accessible, and time-efficient in all directions.

Community Support

From a community perspective, Boobak follows a strong traditional jirga system for dispute resolution. Whenever any social or local issue arises, respected elders and religious scholars come together to resolve matters collectively. Prominent figures involved in this system include Mufti Shams-ul-Haq, Mufti Abdul Haq, Maulana Bagkht Zada, Attaullah Khan Nazim, Rahim Mama, Momin Khan, and several other respected community members who ensure peaceful and fair decision-making
